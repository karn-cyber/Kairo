About This Trek

Kedarkantha is one of the most sought-after winter treks in Uttarakhand. Set amid dense pine forests and wide meadows, the trail takes you through quaint Himalayan villages before reaching the summit at 12,500 feet — offering 360-degree views of peaks like Swargarohini, Bandarpoonch, and Black Peak.

Itinerary

D1
Dehradun to Sankri Base Camp
Road drive approximately 8 hours · Overnight at Sankri base camp · Briefing with guide
D2
Sankri to Juda Ka Talab
Trek 4 km · 3.5 hours · Camp at 9,100 ft · Frozen lake nearby
D3
Juda Ka Talab to Summit Camp
Trek 6 km · 5 hours · Summit at 12,500 ft · Sunrise views over Bandarpoonch
D4
Descent to Sankri
Trek down · Celebration dinner · Certificate distribution
D5
Sankri to Dehradun
Return drive · Drop at railway station · Trip ends

What's Included

Certified mountain guide
All meals on trail
Tents and sleeping bags
First aid and oxygen
Dehradun transport
Forest permits

If Himalayan Trails Co. needs to redirect your booking to a partner operator for any reason, you will receive a notification through Kairo at least 48 hours before your departure date — with full details of any changes.
